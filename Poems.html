“Eighteen Years”


Eighteen years, a bloom in time,
A soul in rhythm, two heart entwined.
You’ve laughed through storms, you’ve wept under the sun,
But oh, my love — look what you’ve become.


You’ve grown through the silence, bloomed through doubt,
Held your light when the world dimmed out.
And I am forever lucky to be standing near,
To see your wonder, so crystal clear.

“If you could see you”


If you could see you through my eyes,
You’d know how stars are poor disguise,
For all the ways you glow at night,
For all the warmth you give to light.


If you could hear your voice the way
It sounds to me, it’s a gentle sway,
You’d understand why even air
Feels jealous when you’re simply there.

“You in every day”


You are the sunshine that brightens the day,
The jacket that warms the coldest haze,
You are the words that bring comfort,
The poetry of each poet’s thought.


You’re why my phone has cheesy notes,
Why I write love in loving quotes,
You’re the “what if” that turned out right,
Oh, my love, what a lovely sight.
